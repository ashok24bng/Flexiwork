Frequently Asked Questions

Simply register for an account, complete your profile, and start working on data entry tasks. You'll earn money for each approved entry.

Earnings depend on your plan:
  • Free Plan: ₹0.50 per entry (max 10 entries/day)
  • Premium Plan: ₹1 per entry (max 50 entries/day)
  • Professional Plan: ₹3 per entry (max 50 entries/day)

You can withdraw your earnings once you reach ₹300. Add your bank details in the wallet section and request a withdrawal. Payments are processed within 24 hours.

Share your referral link with friends. When they join and start earning, you'll receive 10% of their earnings as a bonus in your wallet.
